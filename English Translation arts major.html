About the Major

English / Translation is an academic major that focuses on advanced English language skills and translation between English and Arabic. The program develops students’ abilities in written and oral translation, linguistics, and cultural understanding. Students learn to accurately convey meaning across different contexts and fields. Graduates can work as translators, editors, language specialists, or in media and international organizations.
