We Are a University Students who created the "SANAFER ZUJ" website to help students choose the university major that best suits their interests and abilities.

Our Goal


Our Goal is to guide students in choosing the right university major by providing clear, reliable and helpful information that supports their academic and career decisions.

Project Idea

Many Students struggle with choosing the right university major due to the wide variety of colleges and programs,So we bring all colleges and majors in our university into one platform with clear description , study plans , and essential information to help students make the right choice.
