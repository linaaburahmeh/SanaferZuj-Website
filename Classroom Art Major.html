About the Major

Classroom Teacher is an academic major that prepares students to teach young learners in the early and elementary stages. The program focuses on child development, teaching methods, classroom management, and educational psychology. Students gain practical training to design lesson plans and create an engaging learning environment. Graduates can work in schools and educational institutions, helping children build strong academic and social skills.
