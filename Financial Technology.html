About the Major

Financial Technology (FinTech) is a modern field that combines finance and technology to improve and automate financial services. It focuses on areas such as digital payments, online banking, blockchain, cryptocurrencies, mobile wallets, and financial data analysis. Studying FinTech equips students with skills in programming, financial systems, and data security, preparing them to work in banks, fintech companies, startups, and technology-driven financial institutions.
