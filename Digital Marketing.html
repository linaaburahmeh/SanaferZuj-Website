About the Major

Digital Marketing is a field that focuses on promoting products and services through online platforms and digital technologies. It includes areas such as social media marketing, search engine optimization (SEO), online advertising, content creation, and data analytics. Studying digital marketing helps students develop creative, communication, and analytical skills, and prepares them to work in companies, agencies, or as freelancers managing and growing brands in the digital world.
