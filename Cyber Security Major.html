About the Major

Cybersecurity is a specialized field focused on protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats and digital attacks. It involves securing information, preventing unauthorized access, and ensuring privacy in the digital world. This field includes areas such as ethical hacking, network security, and cryptography. Cybersecurity professionals analyze risks, detect vulnerabilities, and respond to cyber incidents. With the rapid growth of technology, cybersecurity has become essential across all industries.
