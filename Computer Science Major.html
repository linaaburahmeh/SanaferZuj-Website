About the Major

Computer Science is the study of computers and how they work. Students learn programming, algorithms, software development, and problem-solving skills. The major prepares graduates for careers in software engineering, AI, data analysis, and cybersecurity. It combines theory and practical projects to give students the tools to create real-world solutions
