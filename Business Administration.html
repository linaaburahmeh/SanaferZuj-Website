About the Major

Business Administration is a field of study that focuses on planning, organizing, leading, and controlling business activities to achieve organizational goals. It covers areas such as management, marketing, finance, human resources, and operations, and helps students develop leadership, communication, and decision-making skills. Studying business administration prepares graduates to work in a wide range of organizations, start their own businesses, or pursue careers in management and entrepreneurship.
