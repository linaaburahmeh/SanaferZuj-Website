About the Major

Law is an academic major that focuses on the study of legal systems, principles, and regulations. The program covers civil, criminal, commercial, and constitutional law, Students develop strong analytical, research, and argumentation skills, Graduates can work in legal practice, courts, public institutions, and private organizations.
