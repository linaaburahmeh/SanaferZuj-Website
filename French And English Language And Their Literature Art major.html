About the Major

French and English Language and Their Literature is an academic major that focuses on the study of both languages and their literary traditions. The program explores linguistics, grammar, and classical and modern literary texts in French and English. Students develop strong bilingual communication, critical thinking, and literary analysis skills. Graduates can work in education, translation, media, cultural institutions, and international fields.

Achievements

Al-Zaytoonah University of Jordan signed an accession agreement to the Francophone University Agency. The university has been a member of the agency since 2017.
The faculty members and students of the Department and the students participate in the academic exchange programs (Erasmus +) with Saint-Louis University in Belgium.
The students of the Department participate annually in the student exchange sessions of the Arab Council with the universities of the Arab Republic of Egypt
The students of the department have participated in the activity of “The Choice of the Orient”, which is held annually in Beirut, since 2017
The Department obtained accreditation for the French and English Language and Literatures program from the Higher Education Accreditation and Quality Assurance Commission for the academic year 2023-2024.
